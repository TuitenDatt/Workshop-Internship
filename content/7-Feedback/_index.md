### Overall Evaluation

**1. Working Environment**  
FCAJ provides a workplace that is both professional and open, which helps alleviate pressure for new interns. The flexibility and comfortable atmosphere here are key factors that stimulate focus and optimize daily work performance.

**2. Support from Mentor / Team Admin**  
The admin team and instructors closely accompany interns, guiding them toward scientific problem-solving methods. Any difficulties regarding documentation or system workflows are promptly addressed by the seniors, creating a solid support system to ensure interns meet their given deadlines.

**3. Relevance of Work to Academic Major**  
The tasks performed during the internship strictly align with the Information Technology curriculum, specifically in the fields of Cloud Computing and AWS infrastructure. Directly configuring and deploying AWS services not only serves as an opportunity to actualize classroom theory but also broadens the mindset regarding modern technological solutions.

**4. Learning & Skill Development Opportunities**  
Continuous hands-on practice has significantly driven independent research mindsets and deep technical documentation reading skills. At the same time, strict adherence to the work schedule enhances time management capabilities and the readiness to handle infrastructure incidents in real-world scenarios.

**5. Company Culture & Team Spirit**  
The interns here possess a high level of self-discipline and responsibility toward their assigned tasks. Proactive communication and the willingness to share difficulties among members create an efficient internal support network to achieve the best collective results.

**6. Internship Policies / Benefits**  
The enterprise builds a well-structured training roadmap along with transparent company regulations from day one. In particular, the weekly FCAJ Community Day events are a major highlight, creating a valuable academic space to update the latest technology trends.


### Additional Questions
- What were you **most satisfied** with during your internship?  
I was most satisfied and impressed by the opportunity to work directly on live AWS environments through highly practical hands-on labs, alongside the dedicated guidance and mentorship from the managing admin team.
- What should the program **improve** for future interns?  
The organizing team could integrate real-world enterprise problems or specific case studies into the Community Day sessions to give interns a closer look at production project workflows
- Would you **recommend this internship** to others? Why?  
Absolutely yes. This is an ideal environment for students because of its clear learning curve from basic to advanced levels, an expansive space for hands-on experience, and strong backing from experienced seniors.


### Suggestions & Expectations
- I propose that the company diversify the content of Community Day sessions by increasing the focus on real combat experience and architectural solutions in production projects.  
- I desire that the company consider organizing a few casual outdoor or networking activities to strengthen bonding and expand connections among members within the intern community.
